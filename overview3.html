Summary

Networking, or computer networking, is the process of connecting two or more computing devices, such as desktops computers, mobile devices, routers or applications, to enable the transmission and exchange of information and resources. A network component is a hardware or software piece that plays a specific role in connecting devices and ensuring data flow within a network.The seven pieces of network hardware are routers, switches, modems, access points, firewalls, network interface cards, and hubs.
